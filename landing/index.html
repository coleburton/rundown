FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a Strava account?

Yes. Rundown uses Strava as the source of truth so we can keep every goal honest. You connect once during onboarding.

When do the buddy emails go out?

We check your goal every Sunday evening. If you missed your target, Rundown emails each contact with your stats and a nudge.

Is my data secure?

Rundown runs on Supabase with row-level security and encrypted storage. Only your invited contacts see accountability emails.
